ABOUT

Not Yet is a platform where supports the growth mindset, and it is inspired by Carol S. Dweck.
Here we define the so-called "failure" as a working progress --you didn't fail, you just have NOT succeed YET.
Also, here we make a living just by being you, you don't need any specific backgrounds or any titles, you just supply your labor and get the "I OWE YOU" money back.
Remember, we call it "cash flow", that means cash flows, and everybody has a chance to hold money, and money doesn't belongs to or stick to someone. Thus,take the chance to hold the money at your turn.
If your ability could help someone and feed their needs, your work is valuable even though there is no job position in the market.
Forget the market, forget the job description, you have so much potential, and here we help you to discover by you just being you.
